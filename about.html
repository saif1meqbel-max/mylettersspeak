Meet Your Teacher

A Master Educator Who Loves Children.

"Arabic is not a subject to be studied. It is a world to be entered — and every child deserves a guide who knows the way."

The journey began not in a classroom, but at home — seated beside a grandfather who traced Arabic letters into the air with a patient, loving hand. That memory became a mission.

After earning an Ijazah in Arabic Language and spending over twenty years teaching children, the frustration was clear: existing materials either rushed the foundations or failed to engage young minds. MyLettersSpeak was built to change that.

The Method

Different by Design.

Three principles that separate MyLettersSpeak from everything else your child has tried.

01
🔊
Sound Before Sight

Before a child reads a single letter, they learn how Arabic sounds live in the mouth. This phonological foundation prevents mispronunciation habits that take years to unlearn.

02
🏗️
Structure Before Speed

We never rush. Each level is fully mastered before the next begins. Children build genuine understanding — not surface familiarity that crumbles under pressure.

03
📈
Progress Parents Can See

Every milestone is visible and measurable. Parents and children always know exactly where they are, what they've accomplished, and what comes next.
