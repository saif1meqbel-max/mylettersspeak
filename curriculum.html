Innovative Arabic Phonics

My Letters Speak — A Complete Curriculum.

A high-quality phonics programme for Pre-K to first grade — blending the dual approach of whole-language context with precise letter–sound instruction.

The Methodology

Merge, Read & Write, Then Act

The حروفي تتكلم series develops phonological awareness through this signature approach — connecting letter shapes to sounds interactively, then building syllables, words, and confident communication.

Students recognize letter forms, distinguish short and long vowels, and blend sounds into syllables and words. Through stories linked to national identity, they build vocabulary, comprehension, handwriting, and sight words — then express what they learn through drama, movement, and play.

The Book Series

Four Books. One Complete Journey.

Each book in the حروفي تتكلم series targets a different stage of phonics development — from first letter recognition to beautiful Arabic handwriting.

١

My Arabic Letters

Introduces Arabic letters simply — connecting each letter to its shape, picture, and written form.

٢

Drama of Letters

Presents letters in dramatic contexts with writing exercises that promote analysis, synthesis, and evaluation.

٣

Stories of Letters

Educational stories linked to phonics — helping children understand letters through fun, storytelling contexts.

٤

My Journey with Arabic Calligraphy

Teaches the art of Arabic calligraphy gradually — encouraging children to improve handwriting skills step by step.

Curriculum Goals

What Students Will Achieve.

The programme builds foundational literacy through phonological awareness, vocabulary, critical thinking, and confident communication.

🔤Phonological awareness — recognizing and manipulating sounds in spoken language
📚Vocabulary growth through engaging stories linked to cultural identity
💭Critical thinking via inquiry-based comprehension and evaluation
✍️Handwriting fluency through continuous letter, word, and sentence practice
🗣️Communication skills — reading, spelling, and oral expression
❤️A lifelong love of reading through storytelling and creative writing
How It's Taught

Dual Approach & Active Learning.

Combining whole-language understanding with precise phonics instruction — delivered through interactive stations, scaffolded lessons, and multisensory activities.

01

Interactive Activities

Games, story-based lessons, animated letters, and role-play linking sounds to real contexts.

02

Phonics Stations

iPad phonics apps, sound story corners, teacher-led instruction, independent writing, and art & craft.

03

Drama & Expression

Hand gestures for sounds, costumes and puppets for letter characters, and emotional engagement for better recall.

04

Scaffolded Progression

Structured lesson plans moving from general language awareness to precise letter–sound mastery with regular assessment.
