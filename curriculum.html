The Methodology

Merge, Read & Write, Then Act

The حروفي تتكلم book series — حروفي العربية, دراما الحروف, قصص الحروف, and رحلتي مع الخط العربي — develops phonological awareness through this signature approach.

Students distinguish letters by linking shape to sound interactively, recognize all letter forms, learn short and long vowels, and blend them into syllables and words. Along the way they build reading, writing, handwriting, vocabulary, and sight words — gaining confidence in language and communication.
